Suggested Papers for CENPA Journal Club (Spring 2016: Neutrinos)

Neutrino Physics -- Topics and Suggested Papers

Eighty-six years ago, when Pauli proposed the existence of the neutrino, he famously lamented that it was a particle that could never be discovered. Now, neutrino measurements are routine -- and neutrino oscillations were our first hint of beyond-the-Standard-Model physics in the electroweak sector. In Spring 2016, we will survey open topics in neutrino physics.

Neutrino-nucleus interactions/ neutrino detection. Suggested papers:

  • From eV to EeV: Neutrino cross sections across energy scales (Formaggio and Zeller, Rev. Mod. Phys., 2012, 35pp)
  • Measurement of Partonic Nuclear Effects in Deep-Inelastic Neutrino Scattering using MINERvA (Mousseau et al. (MINERvA), arXiv, 2016, 9pp)

Neutrino oscillations. Suggested papers:

  • Neutrino oscillations (Balantekin and Haxton, Prog. Part. Nucl. Phys., 2013, 12pp)
  • New Measurement of Antineutrino Oscillation with the Full Detector Configuration at Daya Bay (An et al. (Daya Bay), Phys. Rev. Lett., 2015, 8pp)

Neutrino mass hierarchy and CP violation. Suggested papers:

  • Prospects for Measurement of the Neutrino Mass Hierarchy (Patterson, Annu. Rev. Nucl. Part. Sci., 2015, 15pp)
  • Neutrino Mass Hierarchy (Qian and Vogel, Prog. Part. Nucl. Phys., 2015, 30pp)
  • Neutrino masses and mixings: Status of known and unknown 3ν parameters (Capozzi, Lisi, Marrone, Montanino, and Palazzo, Nucl. Phys. B, 2016, 17pp)
  • First measurement of electron neutrino appearance in NOvA (Adamson et al. (NOvA), arXiv, 2016, 7pp)

Absolute neutrino mass scale. Suggested papers:

  • Neutrino Masses from the Top Down (Langacker, Annu. Rev. Nucl. Part. Sci., 2012, 20pp) (theory)
  • Current Direct Neutrino Mass Experiments (Drexlin, Hannen, Mertens, and Weinheimer, Adv. High Energy Phys., 2013, 39pp)
  • HOLMES (Alpert et al. (HOLMES), Eur. Phys. J. C, 2015, 11pp)
  • Single-Electron Detection and Spectroscopy via Relativistic Cyclotron Radiation (Asner et al. (Project 8), Phys. Rev. Lett., 2015, 5pp)

Neutrinoless double beta decay. Suggested papers:

  • Neutrinoless double beta decay: 2015 review (Dell'Oro, Marcocci, Viel, and Vissani, arXiv, 2016)
  • Search for Neutrinoless Double-Beta Decay of 130Te with CUORE-0 (Alfonso et al. (CUORE), Phys. Rev. Lett., 2015, 7pp)

Solar neutrinos. Suggested papers:

  • Solar Neutrinos: Status and Prospects (Haxton, Robertson, and Serenelli, Annu. Rev. Nucl. Part. Sci., 2013, 40pp)
  • Results from the Borexino Solar Neutrino Experiment (Calaprice, Galbiati, Wright, and Ianni, Annu. Rev. Nucl. Part. Sci., 2012, 21pp)
  • Neutrinos from the primary proton–proton fusion process in the Sun (Bellini et al. (Borexino), Nature, 2014, 4pp)
  • First Evidence of pep Solar Neutrinos by Direct Detection in Borexino (Bellini et al. (Borexino), Phys. Rev. Lett., 2012, 6pp)

Geoneutrinos. Suggested papers:

  • Geo-neutrinos (Bellini, Ianni, Ludhova, Mantovani, and McDonough, Prog. Part. Nucl. Phys., 2013, 34pp)
  • Spectroscopy of geoneutrinos from 2056 days of Borexino data (Agostini et al. (Borexino), Phys. Rev. D, 2015, 5pp)

Supernova neutrinos. Suggested papers:

  • Supernova Neutrino Detection (Scholberg, Annu. Rev. Nucl. Part. Sci., 2012, 22pp)
  • Physics Opportunities with Supernova Neutrinos (Raffelt, Prog. Part. Nucl. Phys., 2010, 7pp)
  • SNEWS: the SuperNova Early Warning System (Antonioli et al., New J. Phys., 2004, 23pp)
  • KamLAND Sensitivity to Neutrinos from Pre-Supernova Stars (Asakura et al. (KamLAND), 2016, 8pp)

High-energy astrophysical neutrinos. Suggested papers:

  • Search for Prompt Neutrino Emission from Gamma-Ray Bursts with IceCube (Aartsen et al. (IceCube), Ap. J. Lett., 2015, 7pp)
  • High-energy Neutrino follow-up search of Gravitational Wave Event GW150914 with ANTARES and IceCube (Adrian-Martinez et al. (ANTARES, IceCube, LIGO, Virgo), arXiv, 2016, 13pp)

Sterile neutrinos / anomalies. Suggested papers:

  • Light sterile neutrinos: Status and perspectives (Giunti, Nucl. Phys. B, 2016, 18pp)
  • Measurement of the Reactor Antineutrino Flux and Spectrum at Daya Bay (An et al. (Daya Bay), Phys. Rev. Lett., 2016, 8pp)
  • Reactor antineutrino anomaly (Mention et al., Phys. Rev. D, 2011, 20pp)

Exotic neutrino properties. Suggested papers:

  • Neutrino electromagnetic interactions: A window to new physics (Giunti and Studenikin, Rev. Mod. Phys., 2015, 61pp)
  • Global constraints on muon-neutrino nonstandard interactions (Escrihuela, Tortola, Valle, and Miranda, Phys. Rev. D, 2011, 8pp)
  • Neutrino magnetic moment, millicharge and charge radius (Kouzakov and Studenikin, Nucl. Part. Phys. Proc., 2015, 3pp)

Historic neutrino measurements. Suggested papers:

  • Helicity of Neutrinos (Goldhaber, Grodzins, and Sunyar, Phys. Rev., 1958, 3pp)
  • The neutrino: from poltergeist to particle (Reines, Rev. Mod. Phys., 1996, 11pp)
  • The appearance and disappearance of the 17-keV neutrino (Franklin, Rev. Mod. Phys., 1995, 34pp)
  • Discovery of neutrino oscillation:
    Evidence for Oscillation of Atmospheric Neutrinos (Fukuda et al. (SuperK), Phys. Rev. Lett., 1998, 6pp)
    and
    Direct Evidence for Neutrino Flavor Transformation from Neutral-Current Interactions in the Sudbury Neutrino Observatory (Ahmad et al. (SNO), Phys. Rev. Lett., 2002, 6pp)
